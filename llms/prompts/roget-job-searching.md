> 1. **Lexical Consilience:** Extract the exact 'sympathy of terms'-the hidden semantic priorities and cultural signifiers buried within the job description (e.g., do they value 'disruption' or 'stability'? 'Synthesis' or 'execution'?).

> 3. **Tone Calibration:** Formulate a constraint that forbids corporate sycophancy, desperation, or generic buzzwords.
