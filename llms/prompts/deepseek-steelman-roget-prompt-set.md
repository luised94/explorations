The following is a ready-to-experiment Roget-Map Prompt Set. Each variant embodies a distinct region of semantic space by adjusting phrasing, framing, and explicit cues, not just a single word.

---

### Baseline Prompt
**Variant 0 ˙ Neutral reference (coordinate 0,0)**

> Read and analyze the different comments of the paradigm. Steelman the position, convert to positive cristicism and determine how to incorporate each into the guidelines:

*This prompt, taken verbatim, serves as the unsteered control.*

---

### Prompt Variants

#### 1. Fortify
> Examine the paradigm comments with a defensive, evidence-minded eye. For each, **fortify** the underlying stance by appending the strongest supporting facts, precedents, or logical scaffolding you can assemble. Then translate that fortified position into constructive, improvement-oriented feedback and codify how these reinforced insights should reshape the guidelines.

- **Latent dimension(s) primarily modulated:** High evidentiality ˙ heightened urgency ˙ cool, formal tone
- **Steering mechanism:** The instruction "defensive, evidence-minded eye," the demand for "supporting facts, precedents," and the verb "codify" push the model toward lawyerly thoroughness, a need to prove durability, and external validation.

---

#### 2. Distill
> Read the comments, then strip each one down to its irreducible, non-negotiable insight. **Distill** a single, potent core principle. Rephrase that principle as an austere, positive aphorism, and decide how to integrate those concentrated truths into the guidelines without elaboration or excess.

- **Latent dimension(s) primarily modulated:** Very high abstraction ˙ low emotional warmth ˙ very low urgency (contemplative)
- **Steering mechanism:** Terms like "strip down," "irreducible," "austere, positive aphorism," and the prohibition against "elaboration or excess" anchor the model in a minimalist, essentialist mode that seeks elemental clarity.

---

#### 3. Reframe
> Approach the comments not as literal claims but as windows into a mindset. **Reframe** each into a more generous, productive, or psychologically constructive perspective. Express your reframing as appreciative nudges-positive re-descriptions-and explore how these alternative framings can be woven into the guidelines to shift perception.

- **Latent dimension(s) primarily modulated:** Moderate abstraction ˙ diplomatic warmth ˙ low urgency
- **Steering mechanism:** "Windows into a mindset," "generous, productive. perspective," "appreciative nudges," and "woven" cue a psychologically astute, perceptional pivot that values tone over logical architecture.

---

#### 4. Rationalize
> Treat the comments as raw fragments of thought. **Rationalize** each by reconstructing its implied premises, filling logical gaps, and presenting it as a systematically sound argument. Then convert that formalized structure into a cold, clear piece of constructive criticism, and integrate the resulting logical apparatus into the guidelines.

- **Latent dimension(s) primarily modulated:** High abstraction ˙ cold emotional valence ˙ high formality ˙ moderate urgency
- **Steering mechanism:** "Reconstructing implied premises," "filling logical gaps," "systematically sound argument," and "cold, clear" steer the model toward an impersonal, reverse-engineered coherence that prioritizes formal logic.

---

#### 5. Idealize
> Look past all practical constraints, historical accidents, or incomplete expression. **Idealize** each comment into its theoretical optimum-the version that would be true in a perfect world. Articulate that idealized stance as an aspirational tribute, and derive guideline principles that aim for that utopian best, even if it cannot be fully reached.

- **Latent dimension(s) primarily modulated:** Very high abstraction ˙ aspirational warmth ˙ very low urgency
- **Steering mechanism:** "Past all practical constraints," "theoretical optimum," "perfect world," and "utopian best" lift the model into a Platonic register that disregards real-world friction, producing polished, abstract ideals.

---

#### 6. Amplify
> Take the comments and turn up their emotional and rhetorical volume. **Amplify** the urgency, the stakes, and the passion hidden in each stance until it becomes vivid and impossible to ignore. Then channel that heightened energy into a positive, motivating challenge, and inject that charged spirit directly into the guidelines.

- **Latent dimension(s) primarily modulated:** High emotional intensity ˙ very high urgency ˙ low formality
- **Steering mechanism:** "Turn up the volume," "urgency. stakes. passion," "vivid and impossible to ignore," and "inject that charged spirit" push the model toward dramatic, high-arousal language and rhetorical force.

---

#### 7. Charitably Reconstruct
> Approach each comment with the deep assumption that it is wiser and more nuanced than it first appears. **Charitably reconstruct** the most intelligent, good-faith interpretation you can, explicitly noting the interpretative generosity you are applying. Offer your feedback as gentle, encouraging affirmation, and with humility, determine how these generous readings should ripple into the guidelines.

- **Latent dimension(s) primarily modulated:** Low abstraction (concrete interpretation) ˙ very warm emotional valence ˙ low urgency ˙ collegial formality
- **Steering mechanism:** "Deep assumption that it is wiser," "explicitly noting the interpretative generosity," "gentle, encouraging affirmation," and "with humility" create a deliberately slow, empathetic, and self-conscious reconstruction that prioritizes trust and nuance.

---

#### 8. Crystallize
> Scan the comments and force them to declare a single, sharp, unambiguous meaning. **Crystallize** each into a precise, actionable proposition that leaves no room for vagueness. Convert that definitional clarity into blunt but constructive feedback, and define exactly how these knife-edge propositions update the guidelines.

- **Latent dimension(s) primarily modulated:** Low abstraction ˙ high clarity ˙ blunt (low emotional warmth) ˙ high urgency
- **Steering mechanism:** "Force them to declare," "single, sharp, unambiguous meaning," "knife-edge propositions," and "blunt but constructive" demand definitional precision and remove protective ambiguity, steering the model toward operational sharpness.
