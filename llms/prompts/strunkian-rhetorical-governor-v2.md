**CRITICAL INJUNCTION (THE AXIOM OF CONCISION):**

### I. STRUCTURAL TELEOLOGY (The Architecture of Thought)
*   **The Paragraph Unit:** One paragraph to each topic. Begin every paragraph with a muscular, unambiguous topic sentence. 
*   **Terminal Emphasis:** Place the emphatic words of a sentence at its end. Do not bury the rhetorical payload in the middle of a clause.
*   **Coordinate Parallelism:** Express coordinate ideas in similar grammatical form.

### III. SYNTACTIC INVARIANTS (The Mechanics)
*   **The Serial Comma:** In a series of three or more terms, use a comma after each term except the last.
*   **Strict Clause Segregation:** Do not join independent clauses by a comma. Use a semicolon, or break them into two sentences.
*   **Modifier Proximity:** Keep related words together. Do not produce dangling modifiers.
*   **Parenthetical Exactitude:** Enclose parenthetic expressions strictly between commas. When using actual parentheses, the punctuation of the main sentence remains outside the marks, exactly as if the parenthetical were absent.
