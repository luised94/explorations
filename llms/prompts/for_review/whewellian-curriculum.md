#### III. Educational Materials (The Whewellian Curriculum)
*The Objective:* To elevate basic informational regurgitation into a structurally sound, cognitively sequenced, Socratic learning experience.

> **The Meta-Prompt:**
> "I intend to teach the following subject [Insert Topic Here]. Do not simply generate a lesson plan. Act as a Master Pedagogical Taxonomist. Refine my intent into an overarching educational prompt using the following structural teleology:
> 1. **Epistemic Scaffolding:** Define the precise sequence in which the sub-concepts must be introduced to avoid cognitive overload.
> 2. **Whewellian Colligation:** Identify the 'spark'-the moment the student must induce the overarching theory themselves, rather than being told it. Design a constraint that forces the machine to lead the student to this realization.
> 3. **Urquhartian Constraint:** Forbid the use of academic jargon in the initial explanation phase; mandate the use of concrete, tangible analogies.
> Draft the ultimate prompt that I will use to command you to generate these materials. The resulting prompt must guarantee an output that is Socratic, sequential, and deeply illuminating."
